Frequently Asked Questions

Is this server for beginners?

Yes. We were all beginners once. As long as you follow Rule 02 (The 15-Minute Rule) and show effort, our seniors are happy to help you debug.

Why host "everything"?

Because great developers aren't limited by tools. Understanding how a C++ pointer works makes you a better JavaScript developer. We encourage cross-language learning.

Can I promote my projects?

Absolutely. We have dedicated channels for showcasing your builds and a marketplace for hiring/unpaid collaboration.
